# Contributing to Dev Opportunity Radar

First of all, thank you for your interest in contributing to **Dev Opportunity Radar**! 💙

The goal of this series is simple:

> **Help people discover opportunities they otherwise might have missed.**

Each edition is personally researched, written, and curated by me every week. I spend time verifying opportunities, reading through program details, checking deadlines, and adding context so readers understand not just *what* an opportunity is, but *why* I think it's worth sharing.

## 🌟 Community Finds

One of my favorite parts of the series is **Community Finds**.

This section exists because readers share opportunities, communities, resources, events, and programs that others might benefit from discovering.

If you'd like to contribute, I'd love to hear from you.

Some examples include:

* Fellowships
* Grants
* Scholarships
* Internships
* Hackathons
* Conferences
* Startup programs
* Research opportunities
* Learning resources
* Developer communities
* Mentorship programs
* Open-source programs
* Competitions and challenges
* Anything else you think more people should know about

## 📬 How to Share

At the moment, I don't accept pull requests for new opportunities.

Since each edition is personally researched and curated, the best way to suggest something is by leaving a comment on the **latest edition** published on DEV Community.

You can always find the latest edition from the **[README.md](https://github.com/hemapriya-kanagala/dev-opportunity-radar)** of this repository.

If possible, include:

* A short description of what you're sharing
* The official link
* Any important deadlines or eligibility requirements (if applicable)
* Why you think it's worth checking out

A little context goes a long way.

---

## 📝 A Few Notes

* Every submission is reviewed before being featured.
* While I can't guarantee every suggestion will appear in a future edition, I genuinely read every recommendation.
* I generally prioritize opportunities that are currently accepting applications or are expected to reopen in the future.
* If your Community Find is featured, I'll always credit you. If you discovered it, that recognition belongs to you.
